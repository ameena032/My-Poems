THE DIVA LOVE

Love Family

I the diva of my parents, the dainty one
Desire to garner the lofty destiny.
I have seen God in my parents,
What made me so to tell this?

Arms of perpetual love, my mother
The purest love, my mother's eyes.
She holds my hand for a while but heart forever
Her love flows like a gentle flower.

The greatest gift by God, my father
Grabbing his hand is like a covalent bond.
He gives me unwavering support and love,
Guiding light, most affectionate and support of me.

The Love of my parents cannot be measured.
My parents, my stamina, my heart and my soul.
I am so much of what I learned from them
They know how truly, I love them too.

THE BUTTERFLY

Empowerment Society

Butterfly,
Often taken as a smile for girls.
But is that just and what it actually hurls?

Girls are beautiful as butterflies,
With joy of colors in them.
Written on media and heard people believing them.
But in the society?

Is the girl as independent as the butterfly?
Is she allowed to fly away like one?
Does she have the permission to soar the sky so high
like one?

No! Then why this comparison?
Even if forsaken,
Then why don’t we let her be the butterfly of our
society?

Let a girl be independent,
Let her fly high,
Let her get rid of any sigh and attain her goal with her
kind of joy.

Struggle and pain
Comes like darkest way.
Find a way,
Not a place to hide away.

Think about day and night

And hope I like this way

MEMORIES

Nostalgia

In the depths of my mind, memories reside,
A tapestry of moments, forever tied.
They dance and they shimmer, like stars in the night,
Guiding my spirit, with their radiant light.

Oh, the memories I hold, like treasures untold,
Each one a story, waiting to unfold.
The laughter and joy, the tears and the pain,
They all weave together, in a beautiful refrain.

I reminisce on the days gone by,
When innocence and wonder filled the sky.
Childhood adventures, so simple and sweet,
Oh, how I yearn for those memories to repeat.

The flavors of summer, on a sun-kissed tongue,
The warmth of family, love forever strung.
Breezes whispered secrets, as we chased dreams,
Those memories remain, like glowing sunbeams.

The nostalgic scent of a familiar place,
Transporting me back, to a familiar embrace.
Friends long gone, like passing ships at sea,
But their memory lingers, forever with me.

The first love's embrace, an electrifying touch,
Oh, the memories of young love, they mean so much.
We danced together, in a passionate rhyme,
Creating a melody, that stood the test of time.

As seasons changed, and years rolled on,
The memories expanded, like a rolling dawn.
Some faded like whispers, in a distant haze,
While others, like ripples, in my heart, still blaze.

In the kaleidoscope of my mind they dwell,
Those memories, like magic, casting their spell.
They shape who I am, and who I'm meant to be,
For memories in mind, unlock the soul's key.
